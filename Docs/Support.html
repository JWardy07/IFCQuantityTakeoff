What the App Does

  • Imports IFC files from local storage
  • Extracts object counts and available quantity data
  • Reviews materials, storeys, and quantity confidence
  • Exports CSV, JSON, and summary reports
  • Processes model files locally on device

Important Quantity Note

Quantity extraction accuracy depends on the IFC being structured correctly, with rich IFC entity information, object types, material assignments, BaseQuantities, and ElementQuantity data.

IFC Quantity Takeoff supports quantity review and early estimating workflows, but it does not replace professional QS validation.

Privacy

IFC files are processed locally on your device. No model files are uploaded. No account is required. No analytics, tracking, network calls, or third-party SDKs are included.
